Art / Angus Munk

August 13, 2021August 13, 2021 Art Angus Munk

Angus is from London and currently lives in Dalston, while studying furniture design and making at the BCC. Having finished a design degree at Leeds Met he went from Russia to Japan both of which influenced his inspiration and thinking. Noguchi, Kandinsky, Hepworth and Nakashima are a few of his influences and icons.

To see more of Angus’s work, follow him on Instagram.
