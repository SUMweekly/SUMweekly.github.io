    Based in New-York, Wenwen Zhu creates imaginative illustrations which allow the viewer to get lost in the dream-like world that Wenwen has created.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    “My works are like an absurd and gentle elegy, grasping the disorder of life and reconstructing into order. Inspiration comes from life and books. Art creation is a way for me to release emotions. The ecstasy and agony in life are overwhelming. I will record all the moments in my life that touched me, but I am gradually timid about pure happiness and become more honest in the face of sad emotions. When emotions are brewing to be unbearable, I will confide on the paper.”<\/p>\n\n\n\n
