Achingly tender, Stella was a star among seagulls. There were no words to describe the beauty she possessed. Her feathers were still damp from the fountain. She was all white apart from a line of feathers down by her neck that faded from white to grey. A sexy, discreet shade of grey. She stretched out her wings with a flirtatious flutter. She hadn\u2019t imagined the first day after moving to the city would result in meeting a handsome stranger. He was gentle and introspective, qualities Stella admired in a bird. Not like all the cocks she\u2019d met back in Brighton.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Gary had invited her on a tour of London and Stella felt like the unassuming star of a new Netflix rom-com.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n\n\n\n
