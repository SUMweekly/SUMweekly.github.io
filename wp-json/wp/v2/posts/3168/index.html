    Born and raised in San Francisco, Kristen Liu-Wong attended Pratt Institute where she graduated with a BFA in Illustration. their work has been exhibited in numerous galleries on the East and West coasts, along with places in between and beyond. Kristen now lives and works in Los Angeles. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Kristen\u2019s work blends everyday occurrences from her life with abstracted nightmares and crude humour. Trained as an illustrator, she tries to tell a story with every piece she makes, developing a personal and slightly sinister narrative within each painting, using vibrant colours, heavy patterning, and tight compositions. Her work draws inspiration from American folk art, the cartoons she watched as a kid, Shunga, and her appreciation for architecture. She is always striving to make work that is highly personal, yet altered enough to allow individual interpretations to be applied to every story she paints.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
