    We headed down to the Thaddaeus Ropac gallery in Bond street to check out Ron Mueck\u2019s \u201925 years of Sculpture\u2019.<\/a><\/p>\n\n\n\n

    The exhibition debuts some of Muecks\u2019 first work as an artist. After years of producing elaborate puppets and models for television and film he moved on to sculpture when he turned 40, first gaining renown for his work \u2018Dead Dad\u2019, a portrayal of his recently deceased father.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Ron uses hyperrealism while he toys with scale in his extraordinary sculptures. He ventures through the ages, from birth to death expressing dramatic detail in his sculptures visually, whilst highlighting significant moments of his own life.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    The impact and intensity of his craft is evident in the work, with some pieces taking more than a year to create…<\/p>\n\n\n\n
