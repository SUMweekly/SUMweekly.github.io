The non- anatomically grounded style emphasis Julia’s’ observations, on the hands in particular in a \u2018guston like’ inspired manner. Generally smoking or playing sports, the moustached characters leave the viewer guessing, everything but the vague air of discontent and suspicion.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
