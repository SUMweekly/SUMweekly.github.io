    Angela Ho, who works under the moniker Ahoy, was born in Melbourne and is currently based in Hong Kong. Her acid-colored art and illustrations contain characters that detail her reactions to current events and express her inner thoughts within a pop surrealism framework, often with a sense of cheeky humour. Her aesthetic is strongly influenced by vintage advertising art, toys and children’s books, but is made contemporary by parsing these retro attributes through a psychedelic filter.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
