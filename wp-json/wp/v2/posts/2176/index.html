    Vivienne Shao is a Chinese illustrator, born in Guangzhou, China, who now mainly lives in London. Vivienne\u2019s work explores common topics, and her perspectives on them derived from her personal experiences. She creates colourful and fun illustrations, often featuring herself as the protagonist, using highly saturated colours and a sense of humour, to provide a new perspective to the audience.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Vivienne\u2019s recent works often explore female related topics, such as discussing issues around fertility and menstruation. Through these pieces she expresses her thoughts and attitude towards them, in aim of bringing more attention, generating discussion, and pushing to break down the misunderstandings many people hold.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
