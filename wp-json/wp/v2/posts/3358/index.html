    Alana is a figurative painter based in Belfast, Northern Ireland. Staged within a created reality, the artwork is based on exploring childhood, family relationships, and the speculative relationship between real and imaginary.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Her paintings deal with a hyper-reality using both figurative and abstract elements that explore the use of movement and colour to convey ideas of surreal dream-like states and alternative realities. Taking inspiration from cultures, Renaissance paintings and consciousness, primarily based on young people integrated with organic forms.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
