    Jenny Heine takes dreamlike photographs, layered with an abundance of texture and movement. Each shot expertly takes a scene, and re-routes it through a beautiful degree of abstraction. Creating images that convey a feeling of nostalgia, and act as powerful visual metaphors for photography’s role as a parallel to memory itself.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Jenny:<\/span> Although I have always been interested in photography, I only recently picked up a camera. For me, the past couple of years have involved discovering what inspires me. I use black and white because I love its simplicity, minimalistic nature, and the textures and moods I can create all by just changing the settings on my camera. What\u2019s most inspiring, though, is that there\u2019s so much more to learn.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
