    I am Black Childish, an illustrator and animator from Martinique, a french island in the caribbean where I was born and raised. I started playing around with Photoshop when I was a teenager, almost as an accident. I was lucky that my best friend was also very skilled at drawing, so it made me even more interested and passionated about art in general. I also remember that at that time we had a creative mentor, Willtho, who really helped us develop our skills.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    When I turned 18 I moved to Paris to study graphic design, and had a 1 year education degree in motion design, in Gobelins School. This last year was very important for me, not only humanly but also creatively, I could work on very cool projects with a real professional aspect to it, and a certain creative freedom. After my studies I worked as a freelance motion designer for a few years in Paris, then moved to Amsterdam where I joined the animation studio PlusOne<\/a>.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    If I had to describe my illustrations in 3 words I would say – Color, Music, Hip-hop and sometimes Funny!<\/p>\n\n\n\n
