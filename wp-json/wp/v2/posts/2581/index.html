    Huanhuan Wang is a Chinese illustrator currently living and studying at the Royal Academy of Fine Arts of Belgium. Hunahuan makes beautiful, atmospheric illustrations, often characterised by both their delicacy, and suggestive nature. This intent is excellently expressed in their \u201cmini-comics\u201d series, with works that bring the viewer into a timeless space, in which one is asked to make sense of the series of events placed before them.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
