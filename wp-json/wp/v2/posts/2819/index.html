    Wahab Saheed, born 1998, is a Lagos based visual artist. Passionate about creating expressive works, wahab explores the freedom allows, to make statements about the world in which he and his subjects occupy.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    His works are characterized by the use of emphatic mark making and dark values executed in charcoals, with backgrounds painted in bright and cheerful color, serving as a contrast to the emotive and fragile subject matters. Through this he seeks to question the human psychological condition, capturing key events that compose the essentials of human existence. His works oscillate between figurative works, and near abstraction to create a unique establishment for his works.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Wahab attended the Yaba College of Technology where he earned his National Diploma (general art) in 2016, and a Higher National Diploma (painting) in 2019. He has also participated in and won the Alibaba Drawing Dompetition 2020.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
