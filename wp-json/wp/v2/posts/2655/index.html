    Ojo Ayotunde is a Multidisciplinary artist born and based in Lagos Nigeria. As an experimental artist, He employs the use of various mediums of art such as charcoals, oil paints, oil pastels and acrylics to communicate his ideas on a range of subjects. He draws most of his inspirations from an introspective viewpoint, establishing his deep-rooted interest for the personal aspect of human life, the idea of self-awareness and personal growth. He also seeks to shed light on socio-political issues, and shared experiences, in aim of stirring up engaging conversations. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    After obtaining a Higher National Diploma in Graphics design from Yaba College of Technology, he worked as a junior art director with Rosabel Advertising for a year and a half. Since then, he has continued his practice as a full time studio Artist. His works have been featured in art sale and group exhibitions such as The Future Fair (Holiday Market) New York (2020), Domesticity show Vollery Gallery Dubai (2021)\u2026<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    My art is an overall proof of my existence as a human being, navigating and documenting my journey through life. In my recent explorations, in search of a distinct visual language, I have found himself working with palette knives as opposed to paint brushes. This has been an important artistic growth for myself, and I\u2019m constantly looking for new avenues to expand my practice.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
