    Veronica Joseph is a self-taught artist with a background in Interior Design. She was born and raised in Croatia, but has been living in London for the past few years, where she studied at the KLC London School of Design at Chelsea Harbour. Her work can be found in a number of independent magazines, such as Frida Kahlo, Vulva and on the cover of Erica Magazine.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    “I\u2019m constantly inspired by colours and a positive approach to life\u201d. The long-term illness and death of both her parents led her to a unique style of expressionism, whereby she aims to add vibrant and cheerful colours into her work. Her work is inspired by femininity, painting a range of women\u2019s faces in digital format. She does so using various Procreate brushes to experiment with the viscosity, transparency and texture quality of the outcome.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Veronica is passionate about her work, and constantly evolving, in the hope that not only will her art bring people joy, but also that it will inspire people to live a happy and joyous life.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
