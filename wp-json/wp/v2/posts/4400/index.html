    Olanrewaju Joshua is a Lagos based visual artist, who sees art as a conduit for conveying his innermost feelings and emotions to his audience through his emotive painting style.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    His theme of works are driven by his quest or curious interest towards the human identity, its conscious and subconscious cognitive, perceptual, emotive architecture necessary for social life.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    His indistinct and texturized faces act as a metaphor, portraying the disruptive identity of emotional instability caused by social effect. His love for human form and gesture brings his works a figurative character, subverted by an abstractive flair.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    He graduated from the prestigious Yaba College of Education, where he earned his Higher National Diploma (painting) in the year 2019. He has also participated in a selection of workshops and exhibitions in and outside Nigeria.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
