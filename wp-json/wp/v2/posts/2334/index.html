    Stephen Spiers is a Tattoo artist based in Glasgow, his psychedelic tattoos are vibrant and eye-catching, bringing fantasy art into reality.<\/em><\/p>\n\n\n\n

    \n
    \n

    My grandfather had tattoos he\u2019d had done in World War II, as a young child I thought they were great, a pin-up on his forearm that he would make dance by moving his arm; I\u2019ve always remembered that.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    In high school, I would design tattoos for the older kids; it wasn\u2019t difficult to see that this was my main interest as I\u2019d mostly be skipping class to sit in the library reading body modification\/tattoo magazines. Everything else just seemed like a waste of time.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    After a few years at college studying design, I started my tattoo apprenticeship. Back then, apprenticeships were rare and virtually impossible to get straight from school. After four years of apprenticing, I decided to travel, working in various shops around Europe as well as here in the UK.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n\n\n\n
