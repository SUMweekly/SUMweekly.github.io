Art / Azuma Makoto

December 11, 2021December 26, 2021 Art Azuma Makoto

A Bonsai tree suspended at the boundary between earth and space, flowers piled high on an arctic ice shelf..

Azuma Makoto’s botanical sculptures have gained worldwide fame for their attention-grabbing arrangements and extreme locations.

His latest work was the re-imaginging of a 19th century paludarium, used to create a ecosystem to preserve botanical life. Full of technological overkill, Makoto’s paludarium gives us an idea of how life may need to be preserved after the destruction of the natural world…

To see more of Azuma’s work, follow him on Instagram and check out his website.
